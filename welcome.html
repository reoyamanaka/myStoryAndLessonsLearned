My Story and Lessons Learned

My background may or may not be an unusual one. A former pre-med that went rogue and became a software developer and entrepreneur. I'd like to do a statistical analysis on it sometime. I now run a small software business, work at an investments firm as a full stack developer, freelance and occasionally post coding tutorials on YouTube.

I'm blogging about how I made it here and what I'm currently working to accomplish. It's a two-bird-one-stone way for me to reflect on my lessons learned and for anyone interested in entering the dev field, even without a CS-degree, to perhaps take home something useful.
